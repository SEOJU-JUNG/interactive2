Power loss matter RESOUCE DATA : Bitcoin Energy Consumption Index by digiconomist

The increase in power consumption due to Bitcoin is a severe social issue today. In December 2017, Eric Holthaus, a meteorologist, at the news website ‘Grist', he mentioned that by the year 2020 February, the electricity usage of Bitcoin will reach the same amount of the global power consumption. Holthaus, another expert, asserted that the majority of Bitcoin mining is produced in China, and it uses 72% of its electricity generation from coal power plants, which means, in other words, they are burning massive amounts of coal to mine Bitcoin. Digital currency website ‘Digiconomist.’ estimated that 2,31.6 million ton of CO2 annual production is produced due to Bitcoin. According to Digiconomist’s Bitcoin Energy Consumption Index, on February 5th, 2018, 47.26TW amount of electricity is used for Bitcoin mining, which is the same amount of USA’s 43.7 million households power usage.
