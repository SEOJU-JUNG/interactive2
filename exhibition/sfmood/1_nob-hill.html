➀ NOB HILL
With prices as high as its hilltops, immaculately maintained streets and storefronts lend Nob Hill an exclusive air. Historic Victorian homes and spectacular views add to the neighborhood's unattainable mystique, while a Gothic style cathedral contributes to its old-world opulence. Not a neighborhood to be outdone, Nob Hill also boasts world-class restaurants and swanky lounges for after-dinner drinks.
